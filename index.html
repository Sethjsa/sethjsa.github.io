about

I'm Seth Aycock, a PhD candidate in multilingual NLP at the University of Amsterdam. I work with Christof Monz and Khalil Sima'an. My research interests can be summarised by these keywords:
machine translation, multilinguality, low-resource, linguistics, typology, reasoning, data.

I studied Linguistics (BA, Cambridge), and Speech & Language Processing (MSc, Edinburgh). I've worked in multilingual NLP research at Booking.com, Inria Paris, City University London, and the UK Government.
