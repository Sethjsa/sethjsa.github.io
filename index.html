whoami

I'm Seth Aycock, a PhD candidate in low-resource NLP at the University of Amsterdam, where I work with Christof Monz and Khalil Sima'an. My research focuses on machine translation and text simplification in low-resource and out-of-domain settings.

I hold an MSc in Speech & Language Processing from the University of Edinburgh and a BA in Linguistics from the University of Cambridge. Before starting my PhD I worked in NLP research at Inria Paris, City University London, and the UK Government.

My academic interests include machine translation, low-resource and sample-efficient NLP, text simplification, and theoretical syntax.
