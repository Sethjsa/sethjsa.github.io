I take pictures indoors and outdoors, usually on film, on a Canon AE-1 Program. Film stocks used include Portra 400, Fuji 400, Cinestill 400/800T, Ilford HP5, and UltraMax 400. These photos were taken between 2018-now, in: Amsterdam, London, Edinburgh, Cambridge, Prague, Helsinki, Singapore, Iceland, Malta, Chicago, New York, Yosemite, San Francisco, Austin, Seattle, Rocky Mountains, Washington D.C., Baltimore, Belfast, Paris, Seoul, Lombok, Faroe Islands, Isle of Man, and others. I own all the images.
